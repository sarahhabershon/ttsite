About

Terence has been working as a professional sculptor within the film and conceptual arts industry for twelve years. He experiments with modified medical and industrial tools to create small objects and personal adornments that challenge tropes in carving by engaging with unorthodox themes. Terence is currently living and working in Wellington, Aotearoa, carving jade sourced both locally and internationally.
